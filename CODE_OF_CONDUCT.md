# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this
project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression,
level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Respectful communication and constructive disagreement.
- Focused, professional collaboration.
- Clear and honest feedback.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, personal attacks, and exclusion.
- Disrespectful language in code review or discussion.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Report issues to the repository owners. Reporters can expect a timely and
confidential handling process.
